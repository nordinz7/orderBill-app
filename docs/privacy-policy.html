Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Summary: orderBill is an offline-only app. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your information on external servers.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how orderBill ("the App"), developed by Nordin Open Labs ("we", "us", or "our"), handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data Storage

All data you enter into the App — including customer details, orders, payments, invoices, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. We use:

  • SQLite database for customer, order, transaction, and billing data
  • AsyncStorage for app settings and preferences

No data is sent to any remote server, cloud service, or third-party analytics platform.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, process, or have access to:

  • Personal information (names, phone numbers, addresses)
  • Financial data (orders, payments, balances)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics

4. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Contacts access: Used solely to let you import contacts as customers. Contact data is read on-device and never transmitted externally.
  • Storage access: Used for creating and restoring local backups of your data, and for saving generated invoices/receipts as images.

5. Third-Party Sharing

The App includes features to share invoices, bills, receipts, and statements via WhatsApp. When you choose to share:

  • Sharing is initiated only by you — the App never sends data automatically.
  • The shared content is passed directly to WhatsApp on your device. We have no access to or control over what happens after you share.
  • WhatsApp's own privacy policy governs any data processed by WhatsApp.

6. Backup and Restore

The App allows you to export your data as a local JSON backup file. You may choose to share this file via your device's share functionality (e.g., Google Drive, email). This sharing is entirely user-initiated, and we do not have access to your backup files.

7. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, its security is tied to your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend:

  • Keeping your device secured with a PIN, password, or biometric lock
  • Regularly creating backups using the App's backup feature

8. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Delete individual customers, orders, or payments within the App
  • Uninstall the App to remove all associated data from your device

9. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for business use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
